- otto's websocket entry uses `processWebSocketStream(...)` (upstream uses `streamCodexWebSocket`), and `transport` defaults to `"sse"` in otto.

- The `onError`/`onClose`/`onAbort` -> shared `fail()`/`cleanup()` refactor in `connectWebSocket` must be hand-applied to otto's variant.

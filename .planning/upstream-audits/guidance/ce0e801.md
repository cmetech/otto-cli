## Divergence

The upstream fix (serialized write tail + ENOBUFS/EAGAIN/EWOULDBLOCK retry loop + flush-on-shutdown) cannot be cherry-picked because its target module and call sites are absent. This is CRITICAL_STABILITY and supersedes d0d1d8e (do-not-port); the backpressure fix should still be ported, just reconstructed for otto-cli's architecture.

- Replace the direct `process.stdout.write(serializeJsonLine(obj))` in `output()` (line ~54) with the queued/retrying writer.

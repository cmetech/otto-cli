Style: Identical to the reference image. Semi-realistic 3D character illustration in the warm Pixar-adjacent aesthetic. Same soft directional lighting from the upper-left, same fur rendering, same painterly softness, same color saturation, same level of detail. No style drift.

FACE SPECIFICATION — match the reference exactly:
- Front-facing otto portrait, very slight natural head tilt acceptable (no more than the reference).
- Warm chestnut-brown fur in the OTTO Brown family (#8E5A33 range) with subtle directional shading from an upper-left light source.
- Two large round expressive dark eyes (near-black) with a single small white catchlight highlight in each.
- Pale cream/tan muzzle area surrounding a small triangular dark-brown nose with a subtle glossy highlight at the top.
- Small curved cream patches on each side of the muzzle.
- Subtle stippled freckle markings on the cheeks (whisker follicles).
- Light tan brow ridges above the eyes — softer than the surrounding fur.
- Two rounded triangular ears, warm brown outer with darker brown inner.
- Small closed mouth with a slight gentle smile — friendly, slightly mischievous, intelligent.
- Subtle thin whiskers (light cream, almost suggested rather than bold lines).
- Tousled fur texture across the top of the head, identical to the reference.

ISOLATION RULES:
- HEAD ONLY. Crop tight at the base of the neck — no body, no shoulders, no hoodie, no terminal tile, no props, no accessories of any kind.
- No "OTTO" wordmark in the asset (the wordmark sits alongside the face in compositions, not inside it).
- The face is the entire subject. Leave generous negative space at the edges to allow safe-area cropping for circular and rounded-square crops.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- 1:1 square aspect ratio.
- Transparent background (PNG with alpha channel). No drop shadow, no rim light, no background fill, no platform or base.
- High resolution suitable for use from 64×64 (favicon) up to 2048×2048 (large header).
- Clean anti-aliased edges around the silhouette.

DELIVERABLES:
Primary: face on transparent background, square crop, centered.
Optional secondary: the same face composed inside a soft circular badge crop (for social-avatar use), still on transparent background outside the circle.

The result MUST be visually indistinguishable from the reference image's face — same eyes, same nose, same expression, same fur tones, same lighting, same painterly style. The only difference is that the body, hoodie, terminal tile, and brown background are removed.
